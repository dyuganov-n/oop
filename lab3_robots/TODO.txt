Modes
	- manual 
		- move u/d/l/r
		- scan
		- collect
	- scan N
		find closest unscanned cell 
		make steps and scan
		go to manual
	- auto collect
		find closest apple
		make steps and collect
		stop, if all collected
		go to manual

Commands:

- IRobotCommand
	- move u/d/l/r
	- scan
	- collect
	
- IManagerCommand
	- ChamgeModeCommand (manual, scan N, auto)
